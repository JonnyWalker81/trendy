Your Memory Isn't as Good as You Think

We all overestimate or underestimate how often things happen. Without data, it's impossible to know the truth.

The Guessing Game

"I feel like I go to the gym a lot" or "I think I only get migraines occasionally"—but are you sure? Memory is notoriously unreliable, leading to over- or underestimating your habits and symptoms.

Unanswered Questions

Without data, you can't confidently answer: "How many times did I get a migraine this month?" "How often am I actually working out?" "Am I eating out more than I think?"

Manual Journals Are a Chore

Notebooks and spreadsheets are tedious and time-consuming. They're easy to abandon when life gets busy, leaving you with incomplete data or no data at all.

Rigid Habit Apps Don't Cut It

Track What Matters to You

TrendSight adapts to your life. Here are five powerful ways people use it:

Habit Building & Self-Improvement

Log daily habits like reading, meditating, drinking water, or journaling. See your progress fill up over time with visual streaks and weekly summaries.

Before: "I think I'm meditating regularly..."
After: "I can see I meditated 18 out of 30 days this month—time to push for 25!"

Health & Symptom Tracking

Track migraines, mood swings, allergy flare-ups, or digestive issues. Add notes about sleep quality, stress level, diet, or weather to help identify patterns and triggers.

Before: "I can't figure out what triggers my migraines..."
After: "The data shows I get migraines 3 days after poor sleep—now I know what to fix."

Fitness & Workout Analytics

Track gym visits, runs, walks, or yoga sessions. See workouts per week, monthly averages, and identify when you tend to skip—so you can stay consistent and motivated.

Before: "I feel like I'm working out enough..."
After: "I averaged 2.5 workouts per week. Let's aim for 4!"

Lifestyle & Spending Habits

Log events like "ate out," "bought coffee," "ordered delivery," or "late-night scrolling." See how these small habits add up over weeks and months—great for budgeting and awareness.

Before: "I barely eat out..."
After: "Whoa—I spent money on takeout 18 times this month. Time to meal prep!"

Custom Events & Personal Experiments

Track anything: mood scores vs. vitamin intake, deep work hours vs. time of day, social time vs. happiness. Run personal experiments and discover what actually affects your life.

Before: "Does morning sunlight improve my mood? Who knows..."
After: "My mood scores are 30% higher on days I get sunlight before 9am!"

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of events can I track with TrendSight?

Absolutely anything you want! TrendSight is completely customizable. Track habits (meditation, reading), health symptoms (migraines, allergies), fitness (gym visits, runs), lifestyle events (eating out, spending), mood, productivity—you name it. If it's worth tracking, TrendSight can handle it.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your data is private and belongs to you. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your personal events and insights are never shared with third parties or used for advertising.

How is this different from a typical habit tracker?

Most habit trackers force you into predefined habits and rigid checklists. TrendSight is flexible—you create your own event types and track anything that matters to you. It's not just for habits; it's for health, fitness, lifestyle, and personal experiments. Plus, TrendSight focuses on trends and insights, not just checkmarks.

Do I have to be "data-savvy" to use this?

Not at all! TrendSight is designed to be simple and intuitive. Logging an event takes seconds, and the charts are easy to understand. You don't need to be a data scientist—just someone who wants better clarity about their life.

Will there be both iOS and web versions?

Yes! We're launching with a native iOS app and a powerful web app, so you can track and review your data from anywhere—iPhone, iPad, or desktop browser.

Is TrendSight free? Will there be a paid plan?

TrendSight will offer a generous free tier to get you started. For users who want unlimited events, advanced analytics, and deeper insights, we'll offer an optional paid plan. We'll share pricing details closer to launch.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Your data is yours. You'll be able to export your events and insights whenever you want.
