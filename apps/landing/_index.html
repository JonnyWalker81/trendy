FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does automatic health tracking work?

TrendSight integrates with Apple Health (HealthKit) to automatically import your workouts, sleep data, steps, calories burned, mindfulness sessions, and hydration. No manual entry required‚Äîjust connect once and your health data flows in automatically. We only read data; we never write to your Health app.

What are geofences and how do they work?

Geofences are virtual boundaries around locations you visit frequently (gym, office, coffee shop). When you enter or leave a geofence, TrendSight automatically logs an event with timestamps. You set them up once, and tracking happens automatically. Battery impact is minimal thanks to iOS's efficient location monitoring.

What kind of AI insights will I see?

After about 2 weeks of tracking, you'll start seeing: Correlations (connections between events, like "mood is better on workout days"), Patterns (recurring behaviors you might not notice), Streaks (consecutive days of activity), and Weekly Summaries (how this week compares to last). The AI gets smarter the more you track.

Do widgets really let me log without opening the app?

Yes! TrendSight offers home screen widgets (small, medium, and large sizes) plus lock screen widgets. Tap a widget to instantly log an event‚Äîno need to unlock your phone or open the app. Perfect for quick in-the-moment tracking like logging a coffee, mood check, or quick note.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely. TrendSight is offline-first. You can log events, view your data, and use all features without an internet connection. When you're back online, everything syncs automatically. Your data is stored locally and backed up to the cloud.

How is this different from typical habit trackers?

Most habit trackers force you into predefined habits and rigid checklists. TrendSight is flexible‚Äîtrack anything with custom event types and properties. More importantly, we do the work for you: auto-import from HealthKit, auto-log with geofences, and AI-powered insights. Other apps make you check boxes; we help you understand your life.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. Your data is private and belongs to you. We use industry-standard encryption, never share your data with third parties, and never use it for advertising. Health data stays on your device and is only synced with your explicit permission. You can export or delete your data at any time.

Will there be both iOS and web versions?

Yes! We're launching with a native iOS app (with widgets and HealthKit) and a powerful web app for desktop access. Your data syncs across all devices automatically.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Export your events in CSV or JSON format anytime. Filter by date range or event type. Your data is yours‚Äîwe make it easy to take it with you.
